Sector Enforcement Risk - Evidence & Methodology

Sector risk rankings are based on DLA Piper's analysis of the SFO Business Plan 2025/26, which specifically identifies high-risk sectors for FTPF enforcement. The numerical multipliers applied to each sector are indicative weightings reflecting relative enforcement priority, they are not published by any official body and do not constitute legal advice.
SECTOR MULT. EVIDENCE BASIS
Financial Services × 1.5 FCA + SFO dual oversight. Highest enforcement history across all published sources.
— DLA Piper FTPF Guidance 2025
Professional Services × 1.3 SFO actively investigating legal services and accountancy firms.
— DLA Piper; SFO Annual Business Plan 2025/26
Construction & Infrastructure × 1.3 Active SFO investigation — Blu-3 Ltd (April 2025). Named high-risk by DLA Piper.
— DLA Piper FTPF Sector Guidance 2025; SFO Business Plan 2025/26
Energy & Commodities × 1.3 Active SFO investigation into £44m energy fraud (2026). Glencore precedent.
— DLA Piper FTPF Sector Guidance 2025; SFO Business Plan 2025/26
Technology × 1.2 Named as high-risk FTPF sector. Rising fraud exposure in tech supply chains.
— DLA Piper FTPF Sector Guidance 2025
General / Other × 0.9 Baseline. No specific SFO FTPF enforcement signals identified in this sector.
— Indicative baseline only
