FTPF Defence Analyser

A free interactive self-assessment tool for the UK's 
Failure to Prevent Fraud offence under the Economic Crime 
and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (ECCTA 2023, S.199).

## What it does
- 18 questions across all 6 Home Office principles
- Sector-specific prosecution exposure scoring
- Real-time principle heatmap
- Personalised evidence pack status
- Priority action list with timelines
- Print to PDF for board reporting

## Who it's for
Compliance officers, in-house lawyers, CFOs, and General 
Counsel at UK organisations subject to the FTPF offence.
